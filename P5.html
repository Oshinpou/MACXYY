How to Use

  1. Cleanse: Start with a clean, dry face to allow the serum to penetrate effectively.
  2. Apply: Place a few drops of the serum onto your fingertips.
  3. Massage: Gently massage the serum into your face and neck using upward, circular motions.
  4. Absorb: Allow the serum to fully absorb before applying additional skincare products.
  5. Frequency: Use twice daily, in the morning and evening, for best results.
