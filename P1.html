How to Use

  1. Prepare Your Skin: Start with a clean, dry face. For best results, use a gentle cleanser to remove impurities and prep your skin for serum application.
  2. Application: Dispense a few drops of the serum onto your fingertips.
  3. Massage: Gently massage the serum into your face and neck using upward, circular motions. This technique helps promote circulation and ensures even coverage.
  4. Absorption: Allow the serum to fully absorb into the skin before applying your moisturizer or other skincare products.
  5. Frequency: For optimal results, use the serum twice daily, in the morning and evening.
