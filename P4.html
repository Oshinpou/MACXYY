Why You'll Love It

The MACX Pistachio Skin Serum harnesses the nourishing power of pistachios to offer your skin a luxurious treatment. Pistachio oil is renowned for its rich composition of essential fatty acids and vitamin E, which deeply moisturize and rejuvenate the skin. This serum works to enhance skin elasticity, reduce dryness, and leave your skin feeling velvety soft and radiant.

Regular application helps to brighten your complexion and even out skin tone, providing a natural glow. Its fast-absorbing, non-greasy formula is ideal for all skin types, making it a versatile addition to your skincare routine. Experience the transformative effects of pistachios with this indulgent serum that not only hydrates but also protects against environmental stressors.

Powerful Ingredients

  • Pistachio Oil: Provides deep hydration and rich nourishment, improving skin texture and elasticity.
  • Hyaluronic Acid: Locks in moisture, plumping the skin and reducing the appearance of fine lines.
  • Vitamin C: Brightens and evens out skin tone, enhancing overall radiance.
  • Aloe Vera Extract: Soothes and calms irritated skin, providing anti-inflammatory benefits.
  • Squalane: Mimics the skin's natural oils, ensuring optimal hydration and softness.

How to Use

  1. Cleanse: Begin with a clean, dry face to allow for maximum serum absorption.
  2. Apply: Dispense a few drops of the serum onto your fingertips.
  3. Massage: Gently massage the serum into your face and neck in upward, circular motions.
  4. Absorb: Allow the serum to absorb fully before applying your favorite moisturizer or other skincare products.
  5. Frequency: Use twice daily, morning and evening, to maintain hydration and achieve glowing skin.
